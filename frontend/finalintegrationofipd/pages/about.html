About Trashtrek

Trashtrek is dedicated to making waste management smarter and more efficient. We use cutting-edge technology to develop innovative solutions that help individuals, communities, and businesses reduce their waste footprint and contribute to a cleaner and greener planet.

Our story began in 2024 when students of DJ Sanghavi college of Engineering saw the need for a better way to manage waste. We were frustrated by the outdated and inefficient methods that were being used, and we believed that technology could be used to create a more sustainable future.

Since then, we have grown into a team of passionate individuals who are committed to our mission. We are constantly working to develop new and innovative solutions that make waste management easier and more effective.
